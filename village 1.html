Welcome to the village

About Basti Gunjair

Basti Gunjair is a beautiful village located near the Chenab River in Punjab, Pakistan. The village is home to the Jutt Gunjair community and has an estimated population of around 500 people. The residents are known for their hospitality, honesty, and strong community values.

Agriculture is the main source of income, and many families grow wheat, cotton, sugarcane, and vegetables in the fertile lands surrounding the village. The village contains Al Johar School, two mosques, traditional homes, and peaceful rural landscapes.

The Chenab River adds natural beauty to the area and supports agricultural activities. Basti Gunjair represents the true culture of rural Punjab, where people live simple lives, respect their traditions, and work hard to improve their community.

Roots & Heritage

History of the Village

Basti Gunjair was settled generations ago by the Jutt Gunjair clan, drawn to the fertile plains along the Chenab River. Like many villages of central Punjab, its early years were shaped by farming families who cleared land, dug wells, and built homes of mud and brick close to the riverbank.

Over time, the village grew into a tightly-knit community where land, faith, and family ties bind every household together. Elders still recall stories of seasonal floods, harvest festivals, and the building of the village's first mosque — traditions that are passed down at every gathering.

Today, Basti Gunjair stands as a living example of rural Punjab's enduring way of life — simple, dignified, and deeply rooted in the soil it was built on.

Agriculture & Farming

Wheat

Golden wheat fields surround the village, harvested each spring and forming the backbone of local diets and income.

Education & Religious Life

Al Johar School

Al Johar School is the village's center of learning, providing children of Basti Gunjair with primary and secondary education. It plays a vital role in shaping the next generation of farmers, teachers, and community leaders, keeping literacy and opportunity within reach for every family.

Jamia Masjid Gunjair

The main mosque, gathering point for Friday prayers and community events.

Masjid-e-Noor

A smaller neighbourhood mosque serving daily prayers for nearby families.
